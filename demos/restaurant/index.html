Our Story

A Passion for Exceptional Cuisine

Founded in 2010 by award-winning Chef Marco Rossi, Savoria was born from a desire to create a dining experience that celebrates the finest seasonal ingredients while honoring traditional techniques.

Our restaurant combines rustic charm with modern elegance, creating an intimate atmosphere where every guest feels welcome. We work closely with local farmers and producers to ensure that only the freshest, highest-quality ingredients make it to your plate.
