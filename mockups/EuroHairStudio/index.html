About Our Studio

Being the owner of Euro Hair Studio it is always a thrill to create a new look that is on the cutting edge. We provide the highest quality of service and keep up to date on the latest trends, fashions, and products. This allows us to make our clients feel and look fantastic.

Euro Hair Studio is a Full Service Hair Salon that was launched in the Spring of 2011 by Amira and her husband Jasmin Redzic. We believe in having experienced stylists with the knowledge and passion for creating wonderful hairstyles. Our main goal and top focus is hair, that is why we are dedicated to hair fashion. The stylists we chose for Euro Hair Studio are the top in their field and each has something that they have dedicated their career to being the best. We welcome you to come experience us and find out about the best kept secret in Cape Coral.

We hope you come and enjoy our vibrant and relaxed atmosphere. Let us get to work on making you feel and look your best.
